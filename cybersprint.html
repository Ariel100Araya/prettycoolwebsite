Hi there!

Hi! My name is Ariel and this is CyberSprint (pronounced cyber sprint), this was my submission for the Apple Swift Student Challenge in 2026.

The idea

The idea was to make an app that game-ified cybersecurity reflexes, mostly for college students that can deal with these kind of issues quite often. So with different challenges and modes, I made CyberSprint.

Different modes

Standard mode will have you play through a series of cybersecurity challenges in a traditional game format. 10 questions to test your knowledge!

Three hearts mode is a bit more challenging and has a lives system. 3 lives, 10 seconds and you got to try and get as many as possible!

Scaling mode is the hardest and has increasing difficulty levels. Get 5 right? your time limit will decrease! Try to beat your high score!

How do I download CyberSprint?

You can download CyberSprint from the App Store, there is also an Apple App Clip if you just wanna hop on a quick session.
